English Pretest

Please complete this short pretest to assess your current English skills before starting the lessons.

1. Identify the subject in the following sentence: "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog."

2. Which of the following is a synonym for 'benevolent'?

3. What is the correct punctuation for the end of a question?

4. Choose the sentence that uses correct subject-verb agreement.

Your Pretest Results

Week 1: Reading Strategies

Focus: Developing critical reading and annotation skills

  • Active reading techniques
  • Identifying themes and motifs
  • Text annotation methods
Activity: "Short Story Analysis"

Students read and annotate a short story, identifying key literary elements.

Week 2: Writing (PEEL Paragraphs)

Focus: Structured academic writing with evidence

  • PEEL paragraph structure
  • Selecting appropriate evidence
  • Developing explanations
Activity: "Persuasive Paragraph"

Students write a PEEL paragraph arguing a position on a current issue.

Week 3: Speaking & Listening

Focus: Developing confident oral communication skills

  • Public speaking techniques
  • Active listening strategies
  • Constructive feedback
Activity: "Mini-Ted Talk"

Students prepare and deliver a 2-minute presentation on a topic of interest.

Week 4: Creative Writing

Focus: Developing narrative voice and structure

  • Elements of storytelling
  • Character development
  • Descriptive language
Assessment: "Short Story Creation"

Students write an original short story demonstrating narrative techniques.
