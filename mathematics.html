Mathematics Pretest

Please complete this short pretest to assess your current understanding before starting the lessons.

1. What is 3/4 as a percentage?

2. If x + 5 = 12, what is the value of x?

3. What is the area of a rectangle with a length of 8 cm and a width of 5 cm?

Your Pretest Results

Week 1: Number Systems & Operations

Focus: Real-world applications of fractions, decimals, and percentages

  • Review of fractions, decimals, percentages
  • Practical applications in budgeting and discounts
  • MYP-style problem-solving approaches
Activity: "Plan a Virtual Trip"

Students calculate travel costs using percentage discounts and currency conversions.

Week 2: Algebra & Patterns

Focus: Introduction to variables and simple equations

  • Understanding variables and expressions
  • Solving basic linear equations
  • Recognizing and extending patterns
Activity: "Pattern Detective"

Students identify patterns in sequences and create their own pattern challenges.

Week 3: Geometry & Measurement

Focus: Practical applications of area, perimeter, and volume

  • Calculating area and perimeter of complex shapes
  • Understanding volume through 3D objects
  • Real-world measurement applications
Activity: "Design a Dream Room"

Students create a scaled floor plan and calculate dimensions for furniture placement.

Week 4: Data Handling & Probability

Focus: Data collection, representation, and basic probability

  • Designing and conducting surveys
  • Creating various graph types
  • Introduction to probability concepts
Assessment: "Data Analysis Project"

Students collect class data, analyze it, and present findings using appropriate graphs.
