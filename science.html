Science

Shifting from PYP's thematic science to MYP's structured disciplines (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)

Back to Subjects

Week 1: Scientific Method & Lab Skills

Focus: Developing inquiry skills and understanding variables

  • Formulating hypotheses
  • Identifying independent/dependent variables
  • Designing fair tests
Activity: "Paper Airplane Experiment"

Students test how different designs affect flight distance, practicing data collection.

Week 2: Biology (Cells & Ecosystems)

Focus: Microscopic life and ecological relationships

  • Introduction to cell structure
  • Virtual microscope exploration
  • Food chains and webs
Activity: "Build a Food Web"

Students create a digital ecosystem showing energy flow between organisms.

Week 3: Chemistry (Elements & Reactions)

Focus: Introduction to the periodic table and safe reactions

  • Basic element classification
  • Safe chemical reactions
  • Conservation of mass
Activity: "Kitchen Chemistry"

Students conduct safe experiments using household items like baking soda and vinegar.

Week 4: Physics (Forces & Energy)

Focus: Understanding forces and energy transformations

  • Types of forces
  • Simple machines
  • Energy conversion
Assessment: "Rube Goldberg Machine"

Students design a digital model showing energy transfers through a chain reaction.
